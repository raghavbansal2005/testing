CryptoCrystal Metrics

Crypto Crystals, deployed in May 2018, was one of the first Ethereum games. Players can collect and merge crystals of different weights and rarities. Use our app to help identify undervalued stones! We will add features to extend the usefulness of the application based on user feedback; find @amphiboly or @ro4438 in the Crypto Crystals Discord.
